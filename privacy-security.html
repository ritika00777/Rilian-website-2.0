Privacy & Security

Privacy and Security in terms of services and websites generally refer to the protection of personal data and safeguarding user information from unauthorized access.

Key Aspects

  • Data Collection — Informing users of the data collected (e.g., name, email, etc.) and why it's needed.
  • Security Measures — Implementing encryption, firewalls, and secure servers to protect data.
  • User Rights — Giving users control over their data (e.g., access, deletion).
  • Third-Party Sharing — Transparency about whether user data is shared with third parties.
