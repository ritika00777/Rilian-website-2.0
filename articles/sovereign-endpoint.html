Sovereign Endpoint Deployment in One Week: Inside Rilian's Role in a National Security Mission

Rilian 2025

The Mission

A government agency protecting high-value personnel required securing mobile endpoints in a sensitive operational setting. The initiative demanded infrastructure under complete agency control with zero reliance on public cloud services or third-party vendors. Standard SaaS approaches were unsuitable for this context.

Key requirements included:

  • Full sovereignty over infrastructure, telemetry, and data movement
  • Deployment on agency-owned infrastructure with complete visibility
  • Protection of sensitive communications and mobility patterns
  • Advanced Mobile Threat Defense delivered internally

Operational Constraints

The deployment faced strict, non-negotiable restrictions:

  • Air-gapped deployment with no outbound internet access during installation or operations
  • Sovereign data residency keeping all telemetry within national borders
  • No public exposure of services or APIs
  • Hardware-bound execution on bare-metal servers only
  • Internal RBAC enforcement tied to the agency's identity provider
  • SIEM integration enabling telemetry flow into internal monitoring systems

Solution Highlights

Rilian deployed a fully containerized Mobile Threat Defense platform with Desktop Incident Response using DawnTreader — purpose-built to streamline deployment in sovereign, air-gapped, and mission-critical environments.

The implementation included:

  • Bare-metal Kubernetes infrastructure entirely on-premises
  • Signed microservice images scanned for vulnerabilities pre-deployment
  • Air-gapped configuration prohibiting outbound internet access
  • Internal RBAC hardwired to the agency's identity provider
  • SIEM integration for telemetry and audit log flows

Operational Impact

  • Sovereign control preserved end-to-end — all artifacts remained within national borders
  • Deployment completed in one week; full implementation took three weeks
  • Installation and upgrades executed through scripted Kubernetes rollouts
  • Architecture designed for extensibility and future expansion

DawnTreader as Strategic Advantage

DawnTreader enables organizations to adopt mission-critical technologies across air-gapped and classified systems by automating compliance, integration, and delivery through autonomous infrastructure management — while enforcing telemetry, identity, and audit controls by default.

When standard commercial deployment pipelines are off the table, DawnTreader is how organizations move fast without compromising control.
